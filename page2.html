One Color Palette to rule them all

The largest visual difference I have made to SVW has been our color palette. I did some research of where each developer found the right color to use most answered the default color palette in Vuetify or bootstrap. Some even used default css colors.

I worked with some of the marketing team and got our official brand colors and then created variations of each color. I then went through to find all the uses of color and make sure they used our single color palette SCSS file.

Tater 2.0

One of the larger projects that I got to design for at SVW is the second iteration of one of their main products Tater Hub.

Tater Hub is used by many potato sheds to manage complex workflows of sorting potatoes by size and defects.

Guided by competitors, developer time constraints, and customer feedback. I made and presented to stakeholders several iterations from wireframes up to high fidelity mock ups.
